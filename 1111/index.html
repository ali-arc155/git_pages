People are too lazy to go to the bin. And they throw from a long distance, and trash seldom ends up in the trash.

What it does

It catches trash when you throw it. For example, you have a can from Coca‑Cola, you throw it — the bin automatically catches it.

How it works

It looks for trash in the air using machine vision and plans where the trash will stop with AI. After that, it goes to the position where the trash will land.

Why use a moving bin

It helps you to throw out trash without going to the bin and keeps your room tidy.
